About

I’m a full stack software engineer and Drama Desk Award-winning actor (for Dave Malloy's widely acclaimed OCTET, directed by Annie Tippe).

A creative and curious team player by nature, I use my experiences as a performer to always consider the needs of my audience, and am relentlessly driven to create well-rounded, streamlined products that are not only enjoyable, but meaningful to use.

I'm a member of Artists Who Code, a volunteer organization which advocates for creative professionals with non-traditional backgrounds in the tech industry, and a graduate of NYU (BFA), UC San Diego (MFA), and the Software Engineering Immersive at General Assembly.
